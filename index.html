About

Built for preventive, practical health technology.

ALARIS is focused on identifying early, meaningful changes in wellness using everyday biosignals. The goal is not just detection, but creating a system that is understandable, usable, and grounded in real biological and engineering constraints.

Preventive

Designed to surface meaningful changes earlier, before problems become more difficult to act on.

Accessible

Built around practical hardware and clear software experiences instead of unnecessary complexity.

Integrated

Combines bioscience, hardware, and AI into one system instead of treating them as separate tracks.

Project

What each subteam is actively building.

Bioscience

Baselines, evidence, and labeling quality

The bioscience team is grounding the system in biological interpretation, baseline review, and stronger labeling workflows that improve downstream model quality.

  • Annotation rubric refinement
  • Evidence review and biosignal interpretation
  • Safer biological framing for model outputs
Hardware

Wearable sensing and physical integration

The hardware team is turning the concept into something real by refining prototypes, integrating sensors, and improving how the device behaves outside of theory.

  • Prototype iteration and integration
  • Sensor wiring and enclosure refinement
  • Reliability and usability improvements
AI Engineering

Data pipeline, evaluation, and software systems

The AI team is building the infrastructure that converts raw biosignals into usable insight, from preprocessing and storage to experiments and system-level evaluation.

  • Feature extraction and model experiments
  • Database and software workflow development
  • Interpretability and output quality checks
