Mission

ALARIS AI aims to spot early, meaningful changes in wellness using everyday biosignals. We combine a low-cost wearable, privacy-respecting software, and transparent AI so people can act before small issues become big problems.

  • Safety first: decisions favor human oversight and clinical guidance.
  • Evidence driven: models are evaluated on real-world, annotated data.
  • Practical: the experience should be simple, helpful, and low-friction.

What is ALARIS AI?

A holistic platform built from three pieces that work together: a wearable sensor, a mobile app, and an analytics layer that turns signals into clear, actionable alerts.

  • Signals: respiratory rate, heart rate & variability, temperature, movement, and sleep patterns.
  • Pipeline: ingest → clean → feature → model → evaluate → explain.
  • Outcomes: weekly trends, “what changed and why,” and sensible next steps.

Subteams

Biology

Grounds the project in clinical reality and defines what “good” looks like.

  • Specify use-cases & success metrics; document assumptions and risks.
  • Design ethical collection efforts and labeling rubric for model training.
  • Review weekly results; write accessible summaries backed by sources.

Hardware / Integration

Turns parts into a reliable device and makes sure data flows end-to-end.

  • Bring up Raspberry Pi & sensors; budget-aware enclosure and power.
  • Build a simple, secure data path from device → app → backend.
  • Stress test; document wiring, BOM, and assembly checklist.

Coding

Owns the data pipeline, models, and the simple app surface.

  • ETL: schema, cleaning rules, and feature computation.
  • Model: baseline → ablations → tuned candidate; add explanations where possible.
  • Integration: small UI with trends & alerts; metrics dashboard for the team.
